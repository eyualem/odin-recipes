Smashed Potatoes

Smashed potatoes, a rustic twist on traditional mashed potatoes, are a delectable side dish that adds a touch of comfort to any meal. Made by boiling whole potatoes until tender, then gently smashing them with a fork or potato masher, these potatoes offer a perfect balance of creamy interiors and crispy exteriors. Seasoned with butter, garlic, herbs, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper, smashed potatoes are versatile and can be customized to suit any taste. Whether served alongside a hearty roast dinner, grilled meats, or as a tasty snack with dipping sauce, smashed potatoes are sure to delight taste buds and elevate any dining experience.

The ingredients of smashed potatoes are:

  • Boiled Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

The steps for making smashed potatoes are:

  1. Boil Potatoes
  2. Drain Potatoes
  3. Smash Potatoes
  4. Season Potatoes
  5. Bake Potatoes
  6. Serve
